my audio project

In this project I used the software Audacity to Create an Audio Portrait of a situation using sounds. The story I decided to depict in this project was a speeding car getting into a car crash, and I combined a total of seven audio samples and used a reverb effect on one of the audio samples, which was the car alarm audio. The seven audio samples I used consist of a door opening and closing out of a house, footsteps, birds singing in nature, car door opening and car starting, car speeding, car crash and a car alarm. The most challenging part of the project was combining all the sounds together and trying to depict a clear situation due to not having the sounds I precisely needed, which I then solved by cutting the sounds and mixing and matching them to present a clear story.
